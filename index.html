About

Who is Vivien Ducros?

Vivien Ducros is a French entrepreneur who thrives at the intersection of innovation, community, and sustainability. With an insatiable curiosity and a bias for action, Vivien has launched more than 20 side businesses — earning him the label "slasheur serial side businesseur" in the French indie-maker community.


His work spans ed-tech, community living, and impact ventures. He is driven by a single overarching mission: reimagining the human experience — building systems and spaces where people can live with more freedom, connection, and care for the planet.
